Our Favorate Movie

Denreel Monserate

Bridge to Terabithia

I like Bridge to Terabithia because it shows how special friendship can be. Jesse and Leslie’s friendship is really inspiring they help each other feel brave and happy. I also like the magical world they create because it’s fun to imagine adventures. The movie teaches important lessons about being kind, facing hard times, and how imagination can make life better. It’s a movie that feels both fun and touching.

Stephanie Kate M. Fabria

Coco

Coco is my favorite movie because it beautifully tells a story about family, dreams, and remembering the people we love. The movie follows Miguel, a young boy who dreams of becoming a musician even though his family strictly forbids music. The story becomes magical when Miguel enters the Land of the Dead and learns the real meaning of family and legacy.

Watching Coco makes me appreciate my loved ones more, and it inspires me to keep pursuing my passions while staying connected to the people who support me.

Carlene Pearl B. Genetiano

Must be Love
This is my favorite movie because it reminds me so much of my childhood. I used to watch it when I was still a kid, and even now that I’m a teenager, I still watch it. My mom is a big fan of KathNiel, and I eventually became a fan too.

Andrea Paula Reyes

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse
I really like this movie because the animation style is so unique, and it feels like a comic book coming to life. The story also focuses on Miles Morales, who is just a regular teenager trying to figure out who he is. I relate to that part because sometimes I also feel unsure about my choices and what I want to do in life. The movie teaches that anyone can wear the mask, which to me means anyone can be a hero in their own way. The soundtrack is also really good and makes the scenes more exciting.

Godwyne Bacor

Avengers: Endgame

I like Avengers: Endgame because it has lots of action and my favorite superheroes working together to save the world. The movie is exciting and full of cool fights and amazing powers. It also shows how friends can be strong when they help each other, even when things are really hard. I like how the heroes never give up, and it teaches me about teamwork and bravery.
